About

Research at the intersection of sport, people, and technology.

The SPLASH Lab advances research across the sport industry by integrating psychology, learning, analytics, strategy, and human-centered artificial intelligence.

Research

Our Research Areas

Sport Performance & Psychology

Understanding the psychological, behavioral, and organizational factors that influence performance and well-being in sport.

Learning & Leadership

Examining learning, leadership, communication, and decision-making across sport organizations and educational settings.

Analytics & Strategy

Applying data science, analytics, and strategic thinking to solve complex challenges scross the sport industry.

Human-Centered AI

Exploring responsible, ethical, and human-centered applications of artificial intelligence that empower people, organizations, and society.

Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Investigataing emerging technologies, entrepreneurial ecosystems, and innovation adoption in the sport industry.

Fan & Consumer Research

Studying fan experiences, consumer behavior, communication, and engagement in evolving sport landscape.
