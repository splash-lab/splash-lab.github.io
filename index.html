About

Research at the intersection of sport, people, and technology.

The SPLASH Lab investigates how sport performance, psychology, learning, analytics, strategy, and human-centered artificial intelligence can advance sport organizations and human potential.

Research

Our Research Areas

Sport Performance & Psychology

Understanding the psychological, behavioral, and organizational factors that influence performance and well-being in sport.

Learning & Leadership

Examining learning, leadership, communication, and decision-making across sport organizations and educational settings.

Analytics & Strategy

Applying data science, analytics, and strategic thinking to solve complex challenges in sport.

Human-Centered AI

Exploring responsible and meaningful uses of artificial intelligence that support people, organizations, and society.

Innovation in Sport

Studying emerging technologies, entrepreneurial ecosystems, and innovation adoption in the sport industry.

Fan & Consumer Research

Investigating fan experiences, consumer behavior, communication, and engagement in evolving sport environments.
